About

My artistic interests span painting, sculpture, drawing, digital art, and design, and I enjoy exploring a wide variety of styles, mediums, and techniques. Much of my work is colorful and influenced by pop culture, with inspiration drawn from artists such as Andy Warhol and Pablo Picasso. Over the years, I have experimented with acrylic, watercolor, oil-based painting, pointillism, charcoal, ceramics, and digital media and am finding new ways and unique ways to express my creativity.

One of my earliest accomplishments was receiving the "Gift of Art" award at the age of 16 for a still-life painting that now resides in the permanent collection of the University of Rhode Island. Since then, I have continued to develop my skills through projects ranging from pop-art paintings and ceramic sculptures to website design and digital content creation.

I hope to explore themes related to mental health, misunderstood conditions, social issues, and the other related issues. For me, art is both a creative outlet and a way to connect ideas, emotions, and experiences that might otherwise be difficult to express.
